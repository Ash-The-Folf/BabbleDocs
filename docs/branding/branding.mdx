## Guidelines

We encourage innovation and collaboration, but please adhere to the following guidelines when using our intellectual property:

### **Logo Usage**
- **Do not modify the logo** - Never stretch, distort, rotate, or alter the proportions of the logo
- **Maintain clear space** - Always leave adequate space around the logo
- **Ensure visibility** - Maintain sufficient contrast with backgrounds
- **No overlays** - Do not place other graphics, text, or elements on top of the logo
- **Use provided versions** - Only use official logo files from our asset pack
- **Preffered Font** - [IBM Plex Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Sans) italics + bold

### **Brand Attribution**
- **Required attribution** - When using Project Babble technology, please provide appropriate attribution to "Project Babble" with a link to our official repository
- **Naming conventions** - Products built using our technology should use phrasing like "**Powered by Project Babble**" or "**Built with Project Babble**" rather than incorporating "Babble" directly in the product name

### **Legal Requirements**
- **Source First License** - All usage must comply with our source first license terms available [here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Project-Babble/ProjectBabble/refs/heads/main/LICENSE.md).
- **Commercial Applications** - We welcome commercial use of our technology through our partnership program.
